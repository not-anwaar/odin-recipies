Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

Baked Mac and Cheese. Needs no explanation :))

Ingredients

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese
  • salt/pepper to taste
  • 1.5 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp smoked or regular paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease baking dish and set aside. Combine shredded cheeses in large bowl.
  2. Cook pasta 1 minute short of al dente. Drain and set aside in large bowl
  3. Drizzle pasta with olive oil and stir to coat pasta. Set aside to cool while preparing cheese sauce
  4. Melt butter in deep saucepan, dutch oven, or stock pot
  5. Whisk in flour over medium heat and continue whisking for about 1 minute until golden and bubbly
  6. Gradually whisk in the milk/heavy cream until smooth. Continue whisking until bubbles are formed on the surface and continue whisking for another 2 minutes. Whisk in salt and pepper
  7. Add two cups of shredded cheese and whisk until smooth. Add another two cups of shredded cheese and continue whisking until creamy and smooth and thicc ;).
  8. Stir in the cooled pasta until combined and pasta fully coated with cheese sauce
  9. Pour half mac and cheese into baking dish and top with 2 cups shredded cheese mixutre then the remaining mac and cheese
  10. Combine panko, parmesan, butter, and paprika in small bowl and sprinkle over mac and cheese. Bake until golden brown(about 30 minutes). Serve warm
