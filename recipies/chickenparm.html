Chicken Parmesan

Description

An Italian dish consisting of breaded, fried chicken topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese. Often served on a bed of pasta. One of my favorites :3

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, halves, boneless, and skinless
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, for frying

  • Breadcrumb Mixture

    • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
    • 3/4 cup regular breadcrumbs
    • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt or to taste
    • 1/2 teaspoon pepper or to taste

  • Toppings

    • 1 cup marinara sauce
    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
    • 2 leaves chopped basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 ° F

  2. Prepare Chicken: Pound chicken to 1/2 inch thickness

  3. Prepare flour/breadcrumbs: Place flour, eggs, and combined breadcrumbs in shallow plate

  4. Dredge Chicken: Coat chicken in flour, then in egg mixture, then breadcrumbs

  5. Fry Chicken: Heat oil in large skillet or frying pan(between 375-400 ° F). Fry chicken in batches(about 2 at a time) until golden brown

  6. Assemble Chicken: Top fried chicken with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan.

  7. Bake: Bake chicken for about 20 minutes or until cheese is melted. Top with basil and serve
