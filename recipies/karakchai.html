Karak/Masala Chai

Desctiption

Spiced Chai. Nice and creamy and strong thanks to the spices and evaporated milk. Perfect on a cold winter day with a blanket :))

Ingredients(makes 6 cups)

  • 4 Tea Bags(preferably Tetley British Blend)
  • 13 cardamom/elaichi pods, cracked
  • 1 3"-long cinnamon stick
  • 3 Tbsp sugar or to taste
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 cup evaporated milk

Instructions

  1. Bring 6 bups water to a boil. Add tea bags, elaichi, cinnamon, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir in evaporated milk, watching carefully to ensure the tea does not boil over. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until thicc ;), creamy, and caramel in color(about 30 minutes)
  2. Strain tea through a fine-mesh sieve into mugs, discard spices and tea bags.
